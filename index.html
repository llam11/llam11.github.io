Research

My research goal is to develop embodied robots, or agents, that are capable of absorbing and utilizing all kinds of information their surrounding environment throws at them. By leverage all the gathered information from their interactions with the world, I hope robots can make more informed decisions and better carry out planning and control. Thus, I am interested in robotics, reinforcement learning, and multimodal learning.
